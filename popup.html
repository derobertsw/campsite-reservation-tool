ReserveAmerica Auto-Booker

Quick Start Guide
  1. Navigate to the ReserveAmerica campsite page you wish to book and select the desired dates.
  2. Set your desired booking time using the "Target Start Time" dropdown.
  3. Keep this tab active and computer awake to ensure booking attempts are not interrupted!
  4. Click "Schedule for Target Time" to begin at the selected time, or "Start Clicking Now" to begin immediately.
  5. Keep this tab active and ensure your computer does not go to sleep during the process.
  6. For more help, see the documentation.
IMPORTANT for NY State Parks: Peak season (Mar 15 - Labor Day): 8 AM weekdays, 9 AM weekends. Off-season: 9 AM all days.
